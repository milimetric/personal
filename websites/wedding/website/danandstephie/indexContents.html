Welcome to our website. You can RSVP, sign our guestbook, request songs, and stay up to date with our plans around the best day ever. A little explanatory note about our invitations: The envelopes are made of flower seeds, Daphne bush, and recycled paper pulp. You can plant or compost them. Also, we drew the invitations ourselves and had them printed on recycled paper.

(Spoiler alert: the seeds in the envelopes include Red Corn Poppy, Corn Poppy, Spurred Snapdragon, Catchfly, English Daisy, Showy Evening Primrose, Gold Yarrow, Roman Chamomile, Tussock Bellflower, Foxglove, Fringed Sagewort, White Yarrow, Red Yarrow, Monkeyflower, and Creeping Thyme. Plant and cover with just one eigth inch of soil and keep moist until established.)
